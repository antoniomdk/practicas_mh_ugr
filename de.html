Evolución diferencial¶

algorithms.differential_evolution.de(X, y, iters, strategy=<function rand_one>, mut=0.5, crossp=0.5, popsize=50, seed=1)[fuente]¶

Differential evolution algorithm.

Parámetros

  • X – Input data for fitness evaluation.

  • y – Input labels for fitness evaluation.

  • iters – Maximun number of generations.

  • strategy – Mutation strategy function.

  • mut – Mutation factor, also known as F.

  • crossp – Crossover probability for each gene individually.

  • popsize – Initial population size.

  • seed – Seed to feed the random number generator.

algorithms.differential_evolution.rand_one(_, current_idx, pop, mut)[fuente]¶

Mutation function for rand/one strategy.

Parámetros

  • current_idx – Index of the individual to apply mutation.

  • pop – Current opulation (list)

  • mut – Mutation factor, also known as F.

Devuelve

a new mutated candidate

Mutation function for current-to-best/one strategy.

Parámetros

  • current_idx – Index of the individual to apply mutation.

  • pop – Current opulation (list)

  • mut – Mutation factor, also known as F.

Devuelve
